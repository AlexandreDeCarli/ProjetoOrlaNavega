# Product

## Register

product

## Users
The primary users are municipal managers (GMUP - Gestores Municipais de Utilização de Praias, municipal secretaries of IAN, SEPLAN, SETUR, etc.), members of the Comitê Gestor Municipal do Projeto Orla, and citizens of Navegantes (SC) interested in ecological preservation and public works tracking. They require a quick, high-fidelity overview of the coastal management plan's progress.

## Product Purpose
To transition from an opaque, complex spreadsheet of 87 coastal actions to a transparent, beautiful, and interactive real-time monitoring dashboard. Success is defined by public transparency, instant intersectoral searchability, and clear visual tracking of environmental and infrastructural indicators for the municipality.

## Brand Personality
Natural, Professional, Sophisticated. The emotional goals are to evoke confidence, transparency, and a strong sense of local coastal identity.

## Anti-references
- Standard bland enterprise corporate tables.
- Over-saturated, non-harmonic primary color palettes.
- Generic AI-generated scaffolds (e.g., side-stripe card borders, gradient text, excessive uppercase sections).
- Opaque structures that require deep nesting or pagination to see basic details.

## Design Principles
- **Nature-aligned Sophistication**: Let the palette and spacing breathe with coastal tones (sea green, ocean blue, sand) matching Navegantes' natural scenery.
- **Immediate Transparency**: Make search, filters, and status badges extremely clear, avoiding visual noise or decorative-only layouts.
- **Fluid Universal Utility**: Deliver a cohesive user experience that adapts flawlessly from high-resolution desktop screens to touch-first mobile devices.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG 2.1 AA compliance with high-contrast elements (> 4.5:1 for body and labels, > 7:1 for headers), keyboard focus navigation, clear landmark structure, and reduced-motion animations where appropriate.
